Der Alef-Beis Velt – DVD Edition

Bring a beloved classic to life! Der Alef-Beis Velt has been teaching generations of children their א-ב in a warm, engaging way. Now, for the first time, this timeless book has been beautifully transformed into a fully updated 3D animated DVD.

A perfect blend of nostalgia and modern creativity—ideal for everyone, and its a beautiful gift.
