By providing Your personal information to Razor, You consent and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and the associated TermsandConditions, as well as the collection, processing, use, and disclosure of information as set forth in this Privacy Policy and the Terms and Conditions.

If You do not agree with any of the terms of this Privacy Policy and Our Terms and Conditions (including any modifications thereto), You should not provide any personal information to Razor and should exit the Website.  However, this Privacy Policy and Our Terms and Conditions will apply to all of Your information and actions prior to exiting the Website.

The Website is a direct-to-consumer Website offering certain products and services, and is intended for use by adults in the United States age 18 or over.  The portions of the Website that enable information to be collected by or submitted to Razor are not intended for, directed to, or to be used by persons under the age of 18. By using the Website, including registering a Razor product or providing Razor with any information, You represent to Razor that You are 18 years of age or older and that any information You provide to Razor about a third party is for a third party who is 18 years of age or older.

If You are a resident of California, please review Our CaliforniaPrivacyNotice for additional California-specific privacy disclosures and information that supplement this Privacy Policy.

If You are visiting a Razor website for a region outside the United States, please review Our Privacy Policy applicable to that region for detailed information concerning Our collection and use of any personal information.  This Privacy Policy does not apply to any Razor website for a region outside the United States.

  • Computer, Internet and Network Activity Information – such as information obtained from Your use of the Website (g., Your server log information, queries or searches, browsing history, internet protocol address, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information).

In addition, certain demographic information may be collected about employees for regulatory reporting and compliance purposes (e.g., gender, race, and ethnicity).

  • Geolocation Information – such as non-specific geolocation information to identify nearby Razor service centers.
  • Other information – such as information from any communications with Us and other user-generated content submitted by You or on Your behalf to Us through e-mail or other means (e.g., text, images, audio, video or other content that You create).
  • Marketing and promotional information – such as information You provide for the purpose of enrolling or participating in any programs, promotions or contests offered by Razor.

Razor and its authorized representatives, resellers, distributors, service providers, contractors, and third parties obtain these categories of personal information from the following sources:

  • Information obtained by Others. For example, information obtained by others as a result of the use of the Website and other websites or social media sites (e.g., X, Facebook, YouTube, Zendesk, Google, Loqate, etc.) or payment information to our authorized payment processors.

COOKIES, BEACONS, AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGY

Razor’s authorized service providers, contractors and certain third parties use digital tracking tools such as cookies and similar technology when You use the Website (e.g., cookies, web beacons and single-pixel GIF files).  Cookies are small files that are transferred to Your device’s hard drive through Your internet browser that enable the Website’s or an authorized service provider’s or third party’s systems to recognize Your browser and capture and remember certain information. This helps Us to improve Your access to Our Website, identify repeat visitors, help ensure the authenticity of registered users, provide the mechanism for online shopping, ordering and payment, and enable Website traffic monitoring.

We use session-based cookies and persistent cookies. Session-based cookies will last only as long as Your web browser is open and are deleted when You close Your browser. A persistent cookie will last until it expires or until You delete it.  We rely upon authorized service providers, contractors and third parties for these digital tracking tools  (called third-party cookies).  These service providers, contractors and third parties may also use cookies, pixels and other identifiers to collect information that indicates how users interact with the Website (e.g., Google Analytics).  

Using cookies helps Us do a number of things to enhance Your experience on Our Website, including to:

  • identify users and visitors to the Website, including new visitors and returning customers;
  • remember and process the items You have selected for an order;
  • process and save Your preferences for future visits;
  • compile analytics, statistics and reports on Our Website and web traffic activity;
  • offer products and advertising that may be aligned with Your interests; and
  • provide other services related to the Website.

INTEREST-BASED ADVERTISING, THIRD PARTY MARKETING AND ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATIONS

We partner with certain third parties, including social media platforms, that use their own digital tracking tools, such as cookies and pixels, to track Your online activities over time and across Our Website and other websites by collecting information through automated means.  These third parties will provide You with ads based upon your browsing history, interests, activities, and preferences.  As a result, You may see certain ads for Razor on other websites based on prior activities on Our Website.  You may be able to disable these digital tracking tools by changing the settings for cookies on the browser for each device You use to access the Website.  In addition, You may be able to opt-out of their digital tracking tools by changing Your preferences on each social media platform.  These third parties may allow consumers to opt-out of receiving advertisements through the Digital Advertising Alliance – https://youradchoices.com/control – or the Network Advertising Initiative – https://optout.networkadvertising.org/?c=1 .

If You receive electronic promotional materials or newsletters from Us or forwarded on Our behalf, You may unsubscribe from these communications at any time by following the instructions contained in the emails You receive. Even if You opt-out of receiving direct marketing messages, We may still contact You in connection with customer service activities.

DO NOT TRACK INFORMATION

Some browsers have a “Do Not Track” feature that lets You tell websites that You do not want to have Your online activities tracked.  There is currently no uniform browser signal for this feature and no industry standard on how to respond to these signals.  Therefore enabling/disabling the Do Not Track feature in Your browser does not ensure that all websites You visit will respond to the request.

Razor does not recognize or respond to Do Not Track signals at this time.  We will reassess Our approach to responding to Do Not Track signals once an industry standard is established.

Razor does not charge for its SMS or MMS services.  However, Your wireless service provider may apply package deductions and message / data rates to all SMS or MMS messages and rates differ from carrier to carrier.  Please contact Your wireless service provider to inquire about the details specific to Your plan.  Message frequency will vary by user.
